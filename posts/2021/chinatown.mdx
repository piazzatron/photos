This is one of my favorite weekend activities. I grab my X-Pro3 and wander around San Francisco Chinatown, soaking up the sights and sounds of the busy corner markets and men 
off-loading produce from trucks into stores.

It's barely-organized chaos, and actually quite tricky to photograph because there's just so much action.

<InteractiveImage image_id="DSCF4241_thn9dr" />

Despite the noise and crowd, it feels strangely calming?

Something about the anonymity of street photography.

<InteractiveImage image_id="DSCF4266_zvqzdh" />

This batch is all Classic Chrome JPEG, shot on the trusty Fuji 35mm f1.4.
